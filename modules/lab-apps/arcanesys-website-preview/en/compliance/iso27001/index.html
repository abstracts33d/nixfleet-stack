ISO 27001:2022 - Annex A mapping

Annex A controls across A.5 (organisational) and A.8 (technological).

ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Annex A is the cross-sector certification reference. The Annex A controls covered by nixfleet-compliance fall in clauses A.5 (organisational) and A.8 (technological).
